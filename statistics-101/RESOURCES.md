# Statistics & Probability Resources

## Knowledge

- [Book: _Naked Statistics: Stripping the Dread from the Data_ — Charles Wheelan](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/17986361-naked-statistics)
  用生活故事講解統計概念，無痛入門。適用於：所有基礎概念的直覺建立。

- [Book: _The Drunkard's Walk: How Randomness Rules Our Lives_ — Leonard Mlodinow](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/3228275-the-drunkard-s-walk)
  從歷史故事切入機率論，涵蓋蒙提霍爾、貝氏定理等經典。適用於：機率直覺與隨機性思維。

- [Article: "The Lady Tasting Tea" — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lady_tasting_tea)
  費雪（R.A. Fisher）設計的經典隨機化實驗，奠定現代假設檢定的思維框架。

- [Video: "The Central Limit Theorem, Clearly Explained" — StatQuest (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YAlJCEDH2uY)
  StatQuest 系列以視覺化方式講解統計概念。適用於：中央極限定理、常態分布。

- [Online: _Seeing Theory_ — Brown University](https://seeing-theory.brown.edu/)
  互動視覺化統計教材，含機率分布、抽樣、假設檢定等互動模組。適用於：視覺化輔助理解。
